How to contribute!



Step One: Get the official format for FAQ articles.

Step Two: Fill out an article explaining the solution to a common problem. (must have already had 5+ Tickets on the subject)

Step Three: Submit the article to the #sss channel on slack.

Step Four: Wait. If it gets accepted, it will be edited and posted. If not, you will be notified of some potential issues.


If you do not follow guidelines, your article will have a far greater chance not to be accepted!
